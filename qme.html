Quantitative Methods in Economics - Roadmap

Statistics & Econometrics - A Complete Learning Path from Foundations to Research Excellence

1 Mathematics

Linear Algebra

Scalars, vectors, matrices; Square matrices, identity matrix, triangular matrices; Unit vectors; Sparse vs dense matrices; Scalar multiplication; Vector and matrix addition; Matrix multiplication (rules and algorithms); Transpose, conjugate transpose; Symmetric, Hermitian, skew-Hermitian, unitary matrices; Rank, determinant, trace; Matrix inverse; Vector spaces; Basis and dimension; Span; Orthogonality and orthonormality; Linear transformations; Solving systems of linear equations; Gaussian elimination; Gauss–Jordan elimination; LU decomposition; QR decomposition; Eigenvalues and eigenvectors; Diagonalization; Singular Value Decomposition (SVD); Inner and outer products; Quadratic forms; Positive definiteness; Linear least squares

Calculus (Single & Multivariate)

Functions of a single variable; Functions of several variables; Limits; Continuity; Derivatives; Partial derivatives; Gradients; Jacobians; Hessians; Minimum and maximum of functions; Optimization intuition; Unconstrained optimization; Constrained optimization; Lagrange multipliers; Taylor series expansion; Step function; Sigmoid function; Logit function; ReLU (Rectified Linear Unit); Softmax; Cost / loss functions

Optimization Methods

Objective / cost functions; Error functions; Likelihood functions; Gradient Descent algorithm; Batch Gradient Descent; Stochastic Gradient Descent; Mini-batch Gradient Descent

Advanced Optimization & Operations Research

Convex vs non-convex optimization; Linear programming; Integer programming; Constraint optimization; Stochastic optimization; Scheduling problems; Resource allocation; Reinforcement Learning and Operations Research hybrids

Matrix Calculus

Vector differentiation; Matrix differentiation; Gradients of quadratic forms; Hessians of quadratic forms; Jacobians of vector-valued functions; Hessians of likelihood functions; Optimization in matrix form

Numerical Methods

Numerical optimization; Root-finding methods; Numerical integration; Iterative algorithms; Convergence and stability of numerical methods

Measure-Theoretic Foundations

Sigma-algebras; Measure spaces; Probability measures; Measurable functions; Modes of convergence; Almost sure convergence; Convergence in probability; Convergence in distribution

2 Statistical Foundations

Descriptive Statistics

Data types (categorical, ordinal, numerical); Measures of central tendency (mean, median, mode); Measures of dispersion (variance, standard deviation, range, interquartile range); Skewness and kurtosis; Empirical distributions; Frequency tables; Quantiles and percentiles; Covariance and correlation; Outlier detection; Data aggregation and grouping; Visualization basics (histograms, boxplots, scatterplots)

Inferential Statistics - Sampling & Estimation

Sampling methods; Sampling distributions; Point estimation; Properties of estimators (unbiasedness, consistency, efficiency); Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE); Method of Moments; Confidence intervals

Inferential Statistics - Hypothesis Testing

Null and alternative hypotheses; Test statistics; Type I and Type II errors; p-values and significance levels; Power of a test; One- and two-sample tests; t-tests; Z-tests; Chi-square tests; Analysis of Variance (ANOVA)

Non-Parametric Inference

Distribution-free methods; Mann–Whitney U test; Wilcoxon signed-rank test; Kruskal–Wallis test; Kolmogorov–Smirnov test; Permutation tests

3 Classical Econometrics

Linear Regression (OLS)

Simple and multiple regression; Interpretation of coefficients; Dummy variables; Interaction terms; Logarithmic and polynomial transformations; Functional form specification; R² and Adjusted R²; F-test for overall significance

Gauss-Markov Assumptions

Linearity; Exogeneity (zero conditional mean); Homoscedasticity; No autocorrelation; No perfect multicollinearity; The BLUE property (Best Linear Unbiased Estimator)

Diagnostics and Model Validation

Multicollinearity (Variance Inflation Factor, VIF); Heteroscedasticity (Breusch-Pagan, White tests); Robust standard errors; Autocorrelation (Durbin-Watson, Breusch-Godfrey tests); Model specification tests (Ramsey RESET); Residual analysis; Normality of errors (Jarque-Bera test); Influence and leverage points (Cook's distance, Hat matrix)

4 Advanced Microeconometrics

Generalized Least Squares (GLS)

Weighted Least Squares (WLS) for heteroscedasticity; Feasible GLS (FGLS); GLS assumptions; Efficiency gains over OLS under heteroscedasticity or autocorrelation

Limited Dependent Variables

Binary choice models: Logit and Probit; Multinomial Logit and Probit; Ordered choice models; Marginal effects and interpretation of coefficients; Model fit measures (Pseudo-R², likelihood ratio tests)

Sample Selection & Censoring

Tobit models (censored regression); Heckman Selection Model (correcting for sample selection bias); Two-step estimation procedures; Identification issues; Sample truncation vs censoring

Count Data Models

Poisson regression; Negative Binomial regression; Overdispersion diagnostics; Incidence rate ratios; Interpretation of coefficients in count models; Zero-inflated and hurdle models (optional/advanced)

5 Time Series Analysis

Univariate Time Series Models

Stationarity; Unit root tests (ADF, KPSS, Phillips-Perron); Autoregressive models (AR); Moving Average models (MA); ARMA models; ARIMA models; Seasonal ARIMA (SARIMA); Model selection (AIC, BIC); Forecasting and prediction intervals; Residual diagnostics (ACF, PACF, Ljung-Box test)

Volatility Modeling

Autoregressive Conditional Heteroscedasticity (ARCH); Generalized ARCH (GARCH); Extensions: EGARCH, TGARCH; Volatility clustering; Forecasting conditional variance; Model diagnostics and goodness-of-fit

Multivariate Time Series Models

Vector Autoregression (VAR); Impulse Response Functions (IRF); Forecast Error Variance Decomposition (FEVD); Granger causality tests; Cointegration; Vector Error Correction Models (VECM); Johansen cointegration test; Structural VAR (SVAR) models; Dynamic conditional correlations

6 Panel Data Analysis

Core Models

Pooled OLS; Fixed Effects (FE); Random Effects (RE); The Hausman test (FE vs RE); Interpretation of coefficients in panel models; Within and between transformations; Clustered standard errors; Model assumptions and diagnostics

Advanced Panel Models

Dynamic Panel Data models; Arellano-Bond estimator (GMM for dynamic panels); System GMM; Difference-in-Sargan tests (overidentification); Instrument selection; Long panels vs. wide panels; Serial correlation and heteroscedasticity corrections; Panel unit root and cointegration tests; Dealing with unbalanced panels

7 Causal Inference

Frameworks

Potential Outcomes Framework (Rubin Causal Model); Directed Acyclic Graphs (DAGs) for causal identification; The problem of endogeneity; Confounding and selection bias; Counterfactual reasoning; Identification assumptions (Ignorability, Conditional Independence)

Quasi-Experimental Methods

Instrumental Variables (IV / 2SLS); Local Average Treatment Effect (LATE); Difference-in-Differences (DiD); Regression Discontinuity Design (RDD); Fuzzy and sharp RDD; Synthetic Control Methods; Propensity Score Matching (PSM); Balancing and overlap diagnostics; Robustness checks

Complementary Topics

Causal mediation analysis; Sensitivity analysis; External validity considerations; Heterogeneous treatment effects; Limitations of observational data for causal inference

8 Econometrics & Machine Learning

High-Dimensional Inference

Regularization methods: Lasso, Ridge, Elastic Net; Variable selection in OLS; Shrinkage estimators; Handling multicollinearity in high-dimensional data; Bias-variance tradeoff; Sparse vs dense models

Causal Machine Learning

Double Machine Learning (DML) for nuisance parameter removal; Causal Forests for Heterogeneous Treatment Effects (HTE); Targeted Maximum Likelihood Estimation (TMLE); Model-assisted causal inference; Interpretation of heterogeneous treatment effects; Feature selection for causal models

Validation & Model Assessment

Cross-validation in structural and causal models; Training/testing splits for predictive vs causal performance; Overfitting and underfitting diagnostics; Robustness checks; Sensitivity analysis; Out-of-sample prediction accuracy; Model interpretability (SHAP, LIME)

9 Bayesian Econometrics

Bayesian Inference

Prior and posterior distributions; Conjugate priors; Bayesian updating; Credible intervals; Posterior predictive checks; Bayesian hypothesis testing; Hierarchical modeling concepts

Computational Methods

Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC); Gibbs Sampling; Metropolis-Hastings algorithm; Hamiltonian Monte Carlo (optional/advanced); Bayesian VAR models; Hierarchical and multilevel models; Posterior simulation diagnostics; Model selection and comparison (Bayes factors, DIC, WAIC)

10 Big Data & Scalable Analytics

Computational Statistics

Bootstrap and resampling methods; Jackknife; Permutation tests; Monte Carlo simulations; Estimating standard errors and confidence intervals for complex estimators; Parallel computation for simulation-based inference

Big Data Challenges

Statistical significance in massive samples (p-value saturation); High-dimensional data issues; Bias-variance considerations; Distributed computing frameworks for econometrics (Apache Spark, Hadoop, high-performance clusters); Memory-efficient algorithms; Approximate inference methods; Scalable regression and ML models

11 Research Standards & Reproducibility

Identification Strategy

Explicit source of exogenous variation; Internal vs. external validity; Pre-Analysis Plans (PAP); Causal identification assumptions; Clear definition of treatment and control groups; Avoiding specification search and researcher degrees of freedom

Robustness Checks

Placebo tests (fake treatments/dates); Sensitivity analysis; Leave-one-out diagnostics; Alternative model specifications; Subsample analyses; Robust standard errors and clustered inference; Model stability assessment

Scientific Workflow & Reproducible Research

Reproducible computing environments (Docker, Conda); Version control (Git); Pipeline automation (Snakemake, DVC); Dynamic reproducible reporting (Quarto, RMarkdown, Jupyter Notebooks); Automated documentation of datasets and code; Data provenance tracking

Ethics & Transparency

Multiple hypothesis testing corrections (Bonferroni, False Discovery Rate, FDR); Anti-p-hacking protocols; Data anonymization and privacy compliance; Open data and code sharing; Ethical reporting standards; Reproducibility checks prior to submission

12 Tools, Software, Libraries & Modules

Programming Languages

Python (core syntax, data types, functions, OOP, functional programming, error handling); R (syntax, vectors, data frames, statistical functions, ggplot2); MATLAB / Octave (matrix operations, modeling, simulation, numerical computation); SQL (data querying, joins, subqueries, window functions); Bash / CLI scripting

Data Handling & Manipulation

Pandas (DataFrames, series, indexing, grouping, merging, pivoting); NumPy (arrays, linear algebra, broadcasting); SciPy (scientific computing, optimization, statistics); data.table in R (fast table manipulation); dplyr / tidyr (data manipulation in R); HDF5 / Feather / Parquet (efficient data storage); Openpyxl / xlrd (Excel I/O); CSV / JSON / XML parsing; Apache Arrow (columnar in-memory format)

Databases & Big Data Tools

SQL Databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite, MS SQL Server); NoSQL Databases (MongoDB, Cassandra, Redis); Data warehouses (BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift); Data lakes (S3, Delta Lake, Lakehouse architecture); Distributed computing frameworks (Apache Spark, PySpark, Dask); Streaming & messaging (Kafka, Flink); ETL orchestration (Airflow, Prefect, Dagster); Data quality frameworks (Great Expectations); Schema evolution & data versioning; CDC (Change Data Capture)

Data Visualization & Dashboards

Matplotlib (plots, subplots, histograms, scatterplots); Seaborn (statistical visualization); Plotly (interactive plots); Bokeh (web-ready interactive visualization); ggplot2 (R visualization); Tableau (BI dashboards); Power BI (self-service analytics); Looker (business intelligence); Dash / Streamlit (Python dashboards); Plotly Express; Altair; Interactive visualization design principles; Dashboard metrics governance

Statistical & Econometric Packages

Statsmodels (OLS, GLS, ARIMA, statistical tests); Scikit-learn (regression, classification, preprocessing, cross-validation); R base stats; car package in R (regression diagnostics); plm (panel data in R); lme4 (mixed-effects models); tseries, forecast, vars (time series in R); Econometrics toolboxes in MATLAB; Bayesian inference packages (PyMC, Stan, rstanarm, brms); Causal ML libraries (EconML, DoWhy, causalml); ARIMA / SARIMA / GARCH modeling

Machine Learning & Deep Learning

Scikit-learn (classification, regression, clustering, preprocessing, pipelines); XGBoost, LightGBM, CatBoost (gradient boosting); TensorFlow (NN, deep learning, custom models); Keras (high-level neural network API); PyTorch (dynamic computation graphs, deep learning); FastAI (high-level ML abstractions); H2O.ai (automated ML); Reinforcement Learning (TF-Agents, Stable Baselines3); MLflow / Weights & Biases (experiment tracking); Feature stores (Feast); Hyperparameter tuning (GridSearchCV, RandomizedSearchCV, Optuna)

Bayesian Computation & MCMC

PyMC; Stan / CmdStanPy; rstan / brms; JAGS; Gibbs Sampling; Metropolis-Hastings; Hamiltonian Monte Carlo; Bayesian VARs; Hierarchical / multilevel models; Posterior predictive checks; Convergence diagnostics (R-hat, traceplots)

Optimization & Numerical Methods

SciPy optimize (minimize, root-finding); CVXPY (convex optimization); Pyomo (linear, integer, stochastic programming); Gurobi / CPLEX (commercial solvers); Numpy linear algebra (SVD, eigenvalues, matrix inverse); Gradient descent implementations; Stochastic optimization methods; Reinforcement learning optimization routines

Parallel & High-Performance Computing

Multiprocessing and threading in Python; Joblib (parallel computation); Dask (distributed computation); CUDA / CuPy (GPU arrays); TensorFlow / PyTorch GPU acceleration; HPC clusters; Slurm / PBS for job scheduling; Vectorized computations in NumPy / MATLAB

Reproducibility & Workflow Tools

Version control (Git, GitHub/GitLab/Bitbucket); Environment management (Conda, virtualenv, renv for R); Docker / containerization; Snakemake / Make / DVC (pipeline automation); Jupyter Notebooks / JupyterLab; RMarkdown / Quarto; Dynamic reporting; Notebook reproducibility practices; Unit testing (pytest, testthat); Documentation generation (Sphinx, roxygen2)

Text Processing & NLP

NLTK; SpaCy; Gensim; Transformers (HuggingFace); Regular expressions (regex); Tokenization, lemmatization, stemming; Word embeddings; Text vectorization (TF-IDF, CountVectorizer); Sentiment analysis pipelines; Machine translation; Named entity recognition

Miscellaneous / Utilities

OpenCV (image processing); NetworkX (graph analytics); SymPy (symbolic computation); Faker (synthetic data generation); Logging; Profiling and benchmarking; Data serialization (pickle, joblib); API clients (REST/GraphQL); Excel automation (openpyxl, xlwings); PDF / Word processing (PyPDF2, python-docx)
